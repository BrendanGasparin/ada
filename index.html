Ada Lovelace

(1815 - 1852)

Ada Lovelace is recognised as the world's first computer programmer.

Born Augusta Ada Byron in 1815, Ada was the only child to the Romantic poet Lord George Byron and his wife Anne Isabella Milbanke.

Lord Byron was surrounded by rumours of infidelity and even incest. Milbanke left him weeks after Ada was born, taking Ada with her, and a few months later Lord Byron left England forever.
